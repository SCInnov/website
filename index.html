Discover a New Way to Regain Mobility

Learn more about our innovative assistive device and how it can help post-stroke hemiplegic patients improve their elbow extension mobility.

Adaptive Muscle Signal Control

The device is controlled by biological signals from the user's muscles, allowing for personalized and adaptive assistance.

Multiple Stroke Modes

Specific modes cater to different variations of strokes, providing tailored support based on individual needs.

Enhanced User Fit with Soft Robotics

Utilizes soft robotics to easily accommodate an extra 10 pounds and ensure improved user fit for maximum comfort and effectiveness.

Discover the Power of Our Products

Initial Assessment

The first step involves conducting a thorough assessment of the patient's condition, including range of motion, muscle strength, and specific needs for elbow extension mobility.

Customization

Based on the assessment results, our team customizes the assistive device to adapt to the individual's muscle signals and unique requirements for optimal functionality.

Mode Selection

Different stroke variations require specific modes of operation. We help select the appropriate mode that best suits the patient's condition for effective assistance.

Testing and Adjustment

After customization and mode selection, the device undergoes rigorous testing to ensure seamless integration with the user's movements. Any necessary adjustments are made for comfort and efficiency.
